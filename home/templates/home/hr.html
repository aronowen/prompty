{% blocktrans trimmed %} You are the HR Manager at a mid-sized tech company with around 120 employees working across engineering, product, sales, and operations. Next month, the organisation is running its first ever AI Application Day—a full-day internal event where staff can learn about AI tools, experiment with them, and propose ideas for how AI could improve their workflows. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Your task is to draft a clear internal email announcing this event to all staff. {% blocktrans trimmed %} You should also communicate how ideas and experiments from the day will be captured and followed up afterwards, and clarify any expectations around participation, behaviour, data security, and the responsible use of AI tools within the organisation. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} Write a prompt that you would give to an AI assistant to help you produce this email.
